Butyl Products and their business Colleagues Nova Cyte medical company generously gave this trailer worth over £45,000 to a Ukrainian medical company who will soon make it operational. On behalf of the PLAST GB Ukrainian Scouting Organisation in Great Britain we assisted in delivering 40 boxes of urgent medical aid.

Ukrainian children in Colwyn Bay

21th November 2022

Rob Drumgold and Karl von Buren were delighted to be invited to the first bonfire night at Earls Court Community Hub for the Plast Scouts on Saturday night. This organization was created 100 years ago for Ukrainian children not only to have fun, but also to teach them Ukrainian culture, especially when they are evicted from their native country. This summer, a humanitarian aid organization in Ukraine was lucky enough to help this amazing group travel to Colwyn Bay in Wales for a huge summer camp.

We resettled a Ukrainian family in UK

18th October 2022

Today we finally collected from Stanstead airport this beautiful family of Ukrainians fleeing the dreadful war being inflicted on their country. It has taken 6 months to complete all the paperwork for their visas. Mulberry House wedding venue owner Simon George & Manager Richard Kidner kindly agreed to sponsor this family. They have refurbished 2 old offices into a purpose made dwelling where they can live independently. They have also committed to employing Batyr & Natalia within their business to help them get established in their new lives in the UK.

The first humanitarian trip to Ukraine

1th May 2022

Not quite where it all started but our first trip under the 4Ukraine banner. Delivering a much needed pick-up truck filled with a variety of critical aid. We were on the early Eurotunnel on Saturday morning, arrived in Krakow late in the evening, delivered the aid on Sunday morning and then flew back arriving home again on Sunday afternoon. A tough couple of days driving but hugely rewarding. The first time we met Andriy and his family in Lancut, Poland - the start of an important partnership for the 4Ukraine team. At the time we had no idea that this was the start of our friendship and a long collaboration.
