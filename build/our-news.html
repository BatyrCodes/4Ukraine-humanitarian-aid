Medical assistance 2023

16th January 2023

Another busy 10 days for 4Ukraine Humanitarian Aid organising this next truck run to Ukraine. This 4WD truck has been donated by Luba Shepherd & BU Aid to go to Ukraine and assist medical teams. A sailing friend Steven McLean is taking part in his first aid run and not only donating his time but also paying all the travel costs,diesel, hotels and flight home. Thank you so much Steven Mclean. This truck is not only packed with vital medical aid collected from Salisbury hospital in Wiltshire yesterday but sadly we will be delivering 150 body bags which we have been asked for urgently
