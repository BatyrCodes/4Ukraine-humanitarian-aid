Who we are?

4Ukraine Humanitarian Aid is a people organisation. We are a team of volunteers who care passionately about helping the children and people of Ukraine. Since our inception in May 2022, we have developed numerous relationships with aid agencies and volunteer groups both in the U.K., Poland and Ukraine. It is the depth of these relationships that allows us to quickly deliver targeted aid to the places it is needed most. As a non-profit organisation, made up entirely of volunteers, we can guarantee your donations are spent entirely on the purchase and delivery of humanitarian aid.

Our Mission

  • Collection, purchase and delivery of humanitarian aid to Ukraine
  • Collaborating with aid agencies and groups within the UK and Ukraine
  • Helping the children of Ukrainian refugee families within the UK
  • Assisting with the sponsorship and relocation of Ukrainian refugees to the UK

Why not get involved?

If you want to transfer humanitarian aid yourself, we can help you with logistics and delivery! You could even make an aid run to Poland or Ukraine and meet the people who we are so desperately trying to help.
